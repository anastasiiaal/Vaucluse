The Crafts are on display at the Sainte-Praxède Chapel

CMAR PACA is organizing the "Reg'Art" exhibition from December 4 to 12, which will bring together the works and creations of 29 artisans from the region.
In Provence-Alpes-Côte-d’Azur, there are precisely 2,024 artisans who are qualified in Crafts. Ceramists, glassmakers, tanners and even designers ... all have unique skills that deserve to be honored. It is for this purpose that the Chamber of Trades and Crafts of the Provence-Alpes-Côte-d´Azur region ... (CMAR PACA) offers the 11th edition of "Reg’Art", on the theme "Curves and Lights". This exhibition, open to the public, will unveil the achievements, restorations and creations of artisans from the region, specially made for the event.
